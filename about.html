WHO WE ARE

Career Counselling at CareerHues is a compendious development program which has been designed to assist students in making and implementing various informed educational choices. The counselling sessions conducted, aims at career exploration, making career choices, managing career changes, lifelong career development and dealing with other career-related concerns. Our counsellors work with adolescents seeking to explore career options, experienced professionals contemplating a career change, parents who want to return to the world of work after taking time to raise their child, or people seeking employment. It is also offered in various settings, including in groups and individually, in person or by means of digital communication.

We help you identify the factors influencing your career development and help you assess your interests, ability and values by identifying strengths and weaknesses, exploring options, career opportunities, a successful career plan, making the right career decision & best career preparation.

In this era of ever increasing and advanced technology, having a globally accepted degree from abroad secures a bright future ensuring an ever-advancing career profile. We provide various services to obtain admission to popular courses in premium institutions nationally and abroad. Our counselling ensures complete support and guidance right from pre-application processes. Our highly dedicated and committed team strives to achieve excellence in the field of overseas educational consultancy.
